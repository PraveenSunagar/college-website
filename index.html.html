Affiliated to Bagalkot University. Offers UG and PG programs. Focus on academic excellence and discipline. Modern classrooms and laboratories. Student-centered learning environment.Svm Jahagirdar College of BCA was founded in 2013. It is a private institute and is located in Bagalkot, Karnataka. Svm Jahagirdar College of BCA courses are offered to students at the UG and PG levels. The institute offers courses in Full Time mode. Svm Jahagirdar College of BCA offers a total of 2 courses in streams such as Engineering and IT & Software. Some of the popular courses offered at the institute are PG Diploma and UG Diploma and the total seat intake for the available courses is 350. These courses are offered to students across several specialisation such as Computer Science Engineering. Apart from this, the Svm Jahagirdar College of BCA campus has a state-of-the-art infrastructure with all the advanced facilities.
